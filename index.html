OUR MENU

North India is one such region where foods ranging from road side snacks to main course dishes delights everyone.So here we serve you our delicious and irresistible North Indian Food

PANI PURI

Popularly known as "GOLGAPPA" this this dish consists of a puri that is filled with pani made using several spices and fillings made of potatoes.

PAV BHAJI

It is a combination of vegetable curry and bread.The vegetable curry is made from various vegetables such as carrots,cabbage cauliflower,etc whereas the bread commonly knoown as Pav tossed with butter is served along with it.

CHOLE BHATURE

It is a combination of chana masala(spicy chickpeas)and bhatura,a fried bread made from maida.To make it even tastier it is accompaied with sliced onions and green chilles.
